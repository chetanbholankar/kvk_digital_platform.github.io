SATPUDA AGROTECH – 2023

Exhibition Overview

Organized on 20–21 January 2023 at the KVK campus, SATPUDA AGROTECH – 2023 was a district-level agricultural exhibition hosted by Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) Jalgaon Jamod and Satpuda Education Society. The aim was to spread awareness about advanced agricultural technologies and strengthen farmer–research linkages.

Key Highlights

  • 125+ stalls featuring innovations from KVK Buldana-I and PDKV Akola.
  • Focus on dryland horticulture, mechanization, and sustainable practices.
  • Demonstrations on soil health, irrigation systems, and crop management.
  • Women empowerment sessions promoting SHGs and agri-business initiatives.
  • Thousands of participants including farmers, students, and agri-entrepreneurs.

Distinguished Guests

  • Chief Guest: Harishchandra Chopade (Scientist, Buyer Ltd., USA)
  • Chairperson: Krishnaroji Ingle (President, Satpuda Education Society)
  • Rajeshji Ekade (MLA), Alkatai Khandare (ZP President), Dr. Swati Wakekar, Mr. Vikas Jadhao (KVK Head)
