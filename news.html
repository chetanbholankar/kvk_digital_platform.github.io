KVK News Letters

Purpose of the Newsletter

The KVK Newsletter is a vital communication tool to inform, educate, and inspire farmers, stakeholders, and the scientific community with updates, innovations, and success stories from the field.

Highlights

  • Frequency: Bi-annual publication (January & July)
  • Content: Training reports, FLDs, OFTs, Extension activities, Farmer success stories
  • Visuals: Rich with photos, graphs, charts, and infographics
  • Special Features: Seasonal advisories, SAC updates, Government schemes
