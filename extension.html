Extension Activities

KVK Jalgaon Jamod plays a vital role in promoting agricultural sustainability and productivity in the Buldhana district by disseminating relevant technologies and knowledge to the farming community through various extension activities.

Key Extension Activities

  • Farmer Training Programs: Training farmers, women, and rural youth in modern agricultural practices.
  • Frontline Demonstrations (FLDs): On-field demonstrations of new technologies.
  • Field Days and Exposure Visits: Visits to research farms and institutions.
  • Kisan Melas and Exhibitions: Agricultural fairs and exhibitions to showcase technologies.
  • Advisory Services: Personalized consultations and mobile-based advisories.
  • Publications: Distribution of booklets, leaflets, and newsletters.
  • Collaborative Programs: Working with government and NGOs for agricultural development.
