Scientific Advisory Committee (SAC) Meeting

Meeting Overview

The Scientific Advisory Committee (SAC) Meeting is conducted annually to review KVK's progress and receive guidance from scientists, officials, and progressive farmers. The meeting ensures that future agricultural activities are need-based and region-specific.

Recent Meeting Highlights

  • Date: [Insert latest date]
  • Venue: KVK Campus, Jalgaon Jamod
  • Chairperson: Senior Scientist & Head, KVK Jalgaon Jamod
  • Participants: ICAR-ATARI officials, district officers (Agriculture, Horticulture, Animal Husbandry), NGOs, private sector representatives, progressive farmers

Key Discussions

  • Review of Annual Progress and Activity Report
  • Technology dissemination and feedback from FLDs and OFTs
  • Promotion of climate-resilient agriculture
  • Use of digital tools like drones and mobile apps
  • Farmer participation and entrepreneurship development

Recommendations & Outcomes

  • Linkages with FPOs and local departments
  • Customized crop-based training programs
  • Encourage value addition and post-harvest practices
  • Utilize data from demonstrations for scientific analysis
  • Promote awareness about government schemes
