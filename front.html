Front Line Demonstration

Front Line Demonstrations (FLDs) is a unique approach to provide a direct interface between researchers and farmers...

During 2021, KVK implemented 18 FLDs on oilseed, pulses, cereals, horticultural crops, livestock, implements & other enterprises on 102 ha of area covering 406 farmers.
