On-Farm Testing (OFT)

Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) Jalgaon Jamod actively conducts On-Farm Testing (OFT) to evaluate and refine agricultural technologies under real farm conditions. These efforts ensure that research-based practices are suitable, practical, and effective for local farmers.

Objectives of OFT

  • Evaluate research findings under actual farm conditions.
  • Refine and adapt technologies to local agro-climatic zones.
  • Promote farmer adoption of improved and validated practices.

Key Focus Areas

Integrated Nutrient Management

Enhancing soil fertility and yield using effective nutrient strategies.

Integrated Pest Management

Eco-friendly pest control methods to reduce crop losses.

Varietal Evaluation

Testing new crop varieties for performance in local conditions.

Weed Management

Developing efficient weed control practices for better yield.

Farm Machinery

Introducing tools and machines to increase farming efficiency.

Livestock Enterprises

Improving animal husbandry practices for better productivity.
